Data preprocessing steps

1. quaternion1.py (creates npy from vibe)
2. make_dataset2persons.py (creates h5 file)
3. txt2npy.py (Kinect skeleton files to npy)
4. map_root.py (Extracts root)
5. preprocessing2person.py (Makes single dataset by combining all those/creates mask)
6. get_seq_len.py (generates sequnce length mask)
7. make_subset_2_person.py (Oversampling/select classes)
